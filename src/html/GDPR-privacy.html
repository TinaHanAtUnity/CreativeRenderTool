A message from Unity

Your privacy is important to us. While you play, we collect a small amount of information to create a better game experience by personalizing the ads you see.

The only personally identifiable information we collect is about your device (Apple’s Advertising Identifier / Google Ad Identifier (show by platform) and your IP address). Ads allow game makers to let you play for free.

Below you can choose to maintain a personalized experience or have a “generic” experience while you play.

Our Privacy Policy explains how we manage player data.

How it Works As you play, our system tries to predict which ads will be most interesting to you based on the games you play, the device you use, and the country you are in.

This is an example of what we could know from the data we collect: We see that you are using an iPhone 8 and you are playing from Germany. In the past week we’ve seen you 5 times in this game and have shown you 27 ads. You have installed 0 games based on those ads. (This is just an example, not accurate data.)

We do not sell or use this data for any other purpose.

Changing your privacy choice You can access these options at any time by tapping the Info icon after an ad appears.

Device Settings Note that some devices also have options for managing advertising data collection. Check your device settings.

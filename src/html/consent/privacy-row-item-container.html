What do our partners collect (measurement and demographics) Our partners likewise may use your advertising ID and IP address to track what types items you like to buy and build a profile about you according to your preferences and household demographic information. Demographic information may include information like whether you are single or married, have children, approximate income etc. Unity does not have demographic information itself, and we do not personalize your experience based on anything other than how you play games and which games you play. Please visit our third parties’ privacy policies to see how you can get your information and how you can manage the information they have about you.

Measurement may also include the collection of certain information off of your phone like how hard you are pressing the screen on your phone (pressure point measurement). The purpose of our partners collecting such information is to prevent fraud by assessing whether it is a person likely viewing the ad or playing the game (uneven pressure points) or if it is a bot put into the marketplace by a bad actor attempting to dupe ad networks.
